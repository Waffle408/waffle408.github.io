This policy describes how Chris Talbot ("Waffle406", "we", "us") handles information when you visit this website and related project pages hosted at waffle406.github.io and Statsystem.dev.

Information we collect

Most pages on this site are static and do not require you to create an account. We do not intentionally collect personal information through these public pages unless you contact us directly (for example, by email).

Some demos or sub-applications may store data locally in your browser (such as preferences or game state). That data stays on your device unless the application explicitly states otherwise.

Server and hosting logs

Our hosting providers (including GitHub Pages) may automatically collect standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, referring page, and access time. We use this information only for security, troubleshooting, and understanding general traffic patterns.

Third-party services

Pages may link to external sites (for example, GitHub repositories). Those sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for how third parties handle your data.

Internal and demo applications

Several projects listed on this site are hosted internally for security reasons and are not available to the general public. If you receive access to a demo or internal system, additional terms and data-handling practices may apply. Contact us before sharing any sensitive or student-related information.

Children's information

This public website is not directed at children. School-related tools referenced here are intended for use by authorized staff in controlled environments, not for direct collection of information from students through this marketing site.

How long we keep information

Email correspondence is kept only as long as needed to respond to your request or maintain reasonable business records. Hosting logs are retained according to our providers' default retention periods.

Your choices

  • You may stop using the site at any time.
  • You may clear local browser storage for demos that use it.
  • You may contact us to ask about information you have sent us directly.
