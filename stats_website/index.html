Welcome to STATS - Short Term Asset Tracking System

Efficiently manage and track your assets in real time

Track and Manage Your Assets With Ease

STATS is a powerful and intuitive system designed to help organizations keep track of their short-term assets like laptops, tablets, projectors, and more. From asset assignment to maintenance and return tracking, STATS ensures you never lose sight of your assets.

With features such as automatic notifications, asset condition tracking, and detailed reporting, STATS is the ultimate solution for managing your assets efficiently.

Learn More About Our Features

Key Features

Asset Management

Track the lifecycle of your assets, from assignment to return. You can add new assets, assign them to users or departments, and track their condition throughout their usage. STATS ensures every asset is properly recorded and accounted for.

  • Add New Assets: Quickly create new asset entries with essential details like asset name, type, serial number, and location.
  • Assign Assets: Assign assets to users or departments with an expected return date. The system automatically tracks assignments.
  • Track Asset Condition: Monitor asset condition and log repairs, ensuring assets remain in optimal working condition.

Automatic Notifications

STATS keeps everyone on track with automatic reminders and alerts for due dates, overdue assets, and maintenance needs. Stay proactive and avoid asset mismanagement.

  • Due Date Reminders: Receive automatic email notifications when assets are approaching their return date.
  • Overdue Notifications: When an asset is overdue, STATS sends a reminder to both the user and the administrator.
  • Maintenance Alerts: Get alerts when an asset needs maintenance or has been flagged for repairs.

Reports and Analytics

Get detailed insights into asset usage, overdue items, and maintenance schedules with STATS' comprehensive reporting tools. Export reports for deeper analysis and record-keeping.

  • Asset Status Reports: View the current status of all assets (assigned, available, or overdue).
  • Asset Usage Reports: Review how often assets are assigned and for how long.
  • Overdue Asset Reports: Track overdue assets and take action accordingly.

Asset Maintenance Tracking

Keep your assets in top condition with regular maintenance tracking. STATS allows you to log repairs, schedule routine checks, and monitor asset health over time.

  • Log Maintenance: Record all repairs and updates made to assets to maintain a detailed history.
  • Schedule Maintenance: Set periodic maintenance reminders to keep assets functioning at their best.

System Security and Backup

STATS ensures that your data is secure and backed up regularly, so you never lose track of your assets. Role-based access control guarantees that only authorized users can perform critical operations.

  • Role-Based Access: Different user roles (Admin, Manager, User) provide appropriate permissions for each user.
  • Data Backup: Regular backups of all asset data to ensure system integrity.
